Turn daily walks into magical journeys.

Walk in real life to earn Energy, unlock progress through cozy fantasy worlds, complete 7-day journeys, and collect unique stickers along the way.

Download on the App Store

🗺️ Magical Journeys

Explore hand-crafted worlds — from moonlit meadows to starlit highlands — one step at a time.

⚡ Earn Energy

Your real-world steps power your progress. Hit your adaptive daily goal to unlock new tiles on the world map.

🏅 Collect Stickers

Complete journeys to earn collectible stickers for your journal. Each one reflects how you walked.

🔒 Privacy First

No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Your health data stays on your device.

How It Works

  1. Connect Apple Health to import your steps.
  2. StrideQuest sets an adaptive daily step goal based on your history.
  3. Walk to earn Energy — Energy unlocks tiles on a fantasy world map.
  4. Unlock all 7 tiles to complete a journey and earn a sticker.
  5. Start a new journey and keep exploring.

StrideQuest Premium

The free tier includes one full journey. Premium unlocks additional journeys, companion style customization, and more. Available as a monthly or annual subscription.
